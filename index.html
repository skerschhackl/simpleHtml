Feijoa

The feijoa is from the Myrtaceae plant family and is native to Brazil and Uruguay. Feijoas can be considered as either warm climate or subtropical.

Green Kiwi

Kiwis are small fruits that pack a lot of flavor and plenty of health benefits. Their green flesh is sweet and tangy.

Golden Kiwi

Gold kiwis distinguish themselves from common kiwis in color, texture and flavor. The Gold kiwi has bronze toned, smooth, hairless paper-thin skin.

Orange

Orange is a citrus fruit that is quite delicious, juicy with high content Vitamin C. The orange fruit a well as its peel offer many benefits for health.
